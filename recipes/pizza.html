Cast Iron Pizza

Description

The perfect pizza for your movie night.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Pizza Sauce
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni slices
  • Fresh basil

Steps

  1. Pour 1 Tbsp olive oil into cast iron pan
  2. Roll out the pizza dough and allow to rise in pan for 2 hours
  3. Preheat oven to 475*F
  4. Top pizza dough with pizza sauce
  5. Top with cheese, pepperoni and basil
  6. Bake for 10-15 minutes
  7. Enjoy hot with your favorite movie

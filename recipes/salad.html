Pizza

Description

Take an Italian bread topping and turn it into a salad with this delicious bean and vegetable recipe!

Ingredients

  • 2 small tomatoes - diced
  • 1 clove fresh, minced garlic
  • 1 green onion - chopped, (green part only)
  • 8 oz canned white beans
  • 2 Tbsp fresh basil - chopped
  • 1 Tbsp. balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tsp. extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/8 tsp. black pepper
  • 1/4 cup shredded mozzarella
  • 4 cups mixed greens or arugula

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients except salad greens and marinate 5-10 minutes.
  2. Toss with salad greens. Serve chilled.
